The VIP Room NYC is the chic, modern nightclub, inspired by European nightlife, with the sophistication of Paris and the energy of Saint Tropez. With locations in five of the world’s unrivaled party destinations – St. Tropez, Paris, Cannes, Dubai, and now New York, VIP Room has become one of the most popular nightlife destinations. VIP Room, started by Jean-Roch in 1998 in Paris, is famous for its ‘crème de la crème’ clientele, with a worldwide reputation for attracting high end international jetsetters, trendsetters, socialites, and celebrities. Luxury lifestyle meets French decadence at VIP Room to offer an exclusive European nightlife experience in New York with live performances, DJs, and non-stop dancing.
